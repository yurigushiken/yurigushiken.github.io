We tracked how infants aged 7 to 11 months shift gaze during GIVE and SHOW events, using our Eye-Track-ML computer-vision pipeline to map dynamic transitions between faces, bodies, and the toy. For GIVE, infants approach adult-like Face to Face gaze patterns by 10 to 11 months, while for SHOW, which requires understanding of intention, infants do not reach adult levels even by 11 months.

See our [poster](/media/CDS-poster.pdf).
